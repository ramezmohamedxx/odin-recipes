Papa Johns pizza

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp instant yeast
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¾ cup warm water
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 tsp dried oregano

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 220°C (425°F).
  2. In a bowl, mix flour, yeast, and salt. Add warm water and olive oil, then knead until smooth.
  3. Roll the dough into a circle and spread tomato sauce evenly over it.
  4. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese and oregano on top.
  5. Bake for 12–15 minutes, until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
