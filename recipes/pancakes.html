Fluffy Pancakes

Description

These homemade pancakes are light, fluffy, and golden brown. They’re perfect for breakfast or brunch and taste amazing with syrup, butter, or fresh fruit.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract (optional)

Steps

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the dry ingredients: flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, mix the milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients until just mixed (small lumps are fine).
  4. Heat a lightly greased pan over medium heat and pour ¼ cup of batter for each pancake.
  5. Cook until bubbles form, flip, and cook until golden brown.
