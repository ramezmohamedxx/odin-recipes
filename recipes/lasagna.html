Lasagna

Description

This classic Italian lasagna is made with layers of pasta, cheese, and rich meat sauce. Itâ€™s a comforting dish perfect for family dinners.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles.
  2. Cook the beef and mix with tomato sauce.
  3. Layer noodles, sauce, and cheese.
  4. Bake until golden and bubbly.
